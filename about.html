About KaruCart

Bangladeshi craftsmanship, presented with dignity

KaruCart exists to give artists, craftspeople, and collectors a place where handmade work is seen for what it truly is: cultural expression, design, memory, and skill.

Our mission

To bring the art, craft, and design language of Bangladesh to a wider audience through thoughtful curation and premium presentation.

Our promise

Every artwork stays connected to its maker. Artist identity, process, and story remain visible throughout the journey.

What makes KaruCart different

We present handmade works with artist profiles, detailed storytelling, authenticity certificates, and a calm gallery-inspired experience.

Who we serve

KaruCart is for collectors, interior lovers, gift buyers, and cultural audiences who want meaningful objects with beauty, context, and trust.
