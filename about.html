About KaruCart

A platform built to honour Bangladeshi craft

KaruCart connects Bangladeshi artists and makers with people who value beauty, provenance, and meaningful objects for living spaces.

Our vision

To present Bangladeshi craft and art with the dignity, visibility, and international-standard presentation it deserves.

Our promise

Artists are never hidden behind inventory. Their names, philosophies, and process remain visible throughout the buying journey.

What makes this different

Careful curation, certificate records, and rich detail pages present each piece with the depth it deserves.

Future growth

KaruCart continues to grow through new collections, artist partnerships, commissions, interior collaborations, and collector services.
