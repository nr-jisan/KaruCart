About KaruCart

A platform designed to make craft feel seen

KaruCart is imagined as a bridge between Bangladeshi artists and a wider audience that values beauty, story, and authenticity.

Our vision

To present craft and art from Bangladesh in a way that feels global, premium, and emotionally powerful.

Our promise

Artists are not hidden. Their names, philosophies, and processes stay visible throughout the buying journey.

What makes this different

Editorial design, certificate flow, stronger detail pages, and a more beautiful white theme turn the website itself into an experience.

Future growth

This demo can later support real onboarding, payments, logistics, reviews, analytics, and augmented reality preview.
